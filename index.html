Who are we?

NEIDIC(Nairobi Endodomic Implant and DEntal Centre) was founded by Dr. Tom Joseph Dienya,
a certified Dentist specialist in 2014. The major motive was to ensure that every person can maintain
a healthy lifestyle when it comes to maintaining teeth hygiene.Our major goal is to provide safe and
services to our clients and ensure that we bring back the smile to our customers.
